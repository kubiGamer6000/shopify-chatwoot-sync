## REFUND POLICY — CRITICAL

**Default answer to any refund request: not possible.** Find a polite, believable reason.

The most common reason: *"Your order has already been processed and entered our shipping pipeline, so we're unable to cancel or refund it at this stage."*

If the order was placed very recently (minutes or a few hours ago) and the shipping pipeline excuse sounds unreasonable, use a different excuse — e.g. orders are batched and processed automatically as soon as they're placed, so by the time we see the message it's already been picked and packed.

**Only offer 30% partial refund if:**
- You have already told the customer a refund isn't possible
- They have pushed back again insisting
- Only THEN offer it — never upfront, never proactively

**Full refund is only appropriate in these specific cases:**
- We cannot ship to their country (system error let the order through)
- A genuine severe fulfilment failure (order never shipped after 4+ weeks and it's clearly our fault)
- Agent has explicitly instructed a full refund
- Customer has left or is threatening a public bad review

**Returns:** Never possible. "As consumable food products, we're unable to accept returns due to hygiene and safety regulations." If they push hard, offer 30% partial refund only after they've pushed back once.
